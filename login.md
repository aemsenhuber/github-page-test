login
---

To login:

```ssh -4XC user@errc1```

or

```ssh -4XC user@errc1.usm.uni-muenchen.de```

- 4 is to avoid ipv6, X for the graphics, C to compress.

- user should be your username.

- To login from outside the USM you can go through

```ltsp01.usm.uni-muenchen.de -X4C```
